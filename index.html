Frequently Asked Questions

Accordion-style FAQ covering Insurance, Protection and Investments. If an answer doesn’t help, users are directed to the Scottish Widows contract page.

Insurance

What is covered by my insurance policy? ▶
Coverage depends on your selected policy and optional benefits. Core coverage typically includes defined events listed in your policy schedule, with exclusions in the terms.

How do I make a claim? ▶
Start a claim by contacting support with your policy number, date of event, and supporting documents. Claims are then assessed against your policy terms.

Protection

What is the difference between life cover and critical illness cover? ▶
Life cover usually pays out on death during the policy term. Critical illness cover pays out if you’re diagnosed with a specified serious condition that meets policy definitions.
Did this answer help?
Can I change my level of protection later? ▶
Some policies allow changes (for example, increasing or reducing cover), subject to underwriting and product rules at the time of change.

Investments

How is investment risk explained? ▶
Investment options are normally grouped by risk profile. Higher potential returns can come with higher volatility and potential loss, depending on market conditions.
